Cutting Weight for Combat Athletes


Website Design & Development

This website is a personal project that I created to help the athletes who I coach in nutrition and weight cutting. The goal was to provide an all-in-one resource that would take a complex and often stressful topic, and make it easy to learn nutrition principles and determine individual dietary needs.

I created a no-nonsense layout that would still present information in an engaging way. Many sections are text-heavy, so I did a lot to break them up visually, and make content easier to digest. I made use of tables, lists, and subsections wherever possible.

A very useful feature of this site is the calculators, which are done with javascript. They let you input certain varibles, and spits out the desired result. This is really important to the functionality of the site, because it gives users concrete numbers to build their diet around, rather than being purely theoretical.

All said, it's a simple website that delivers a ton of value and content without being overwhelming.

Visit the website at jesseconaway.github.io/weightcut
