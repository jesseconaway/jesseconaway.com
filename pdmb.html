Polkadots & Moonbeams

Logo Design

Polkadots and Moonbeams is an online boutique headquartered in Rolla, Missouri. They provide handmade gifts, commemoratives, and keepsakes centered around newborns and their families.

The owner was renovating and updating the site, and wanted an updated look. The goal was to convey a sense of elegance and attention to detail, as well as the feeling of quality that one gets when they hold a handmade piece.

With each of these design options, line weight stays consistent for the sake of continuity, quality, and attention to detail. I used texture to give the sense that you could hold or actually feel the design.
